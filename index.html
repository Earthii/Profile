My Story

I am a software developer who's currently focusing in Front-End Development. I believe that Front-End development plays a crucial part when creating a website since it is the first thing the client will see. It is the main component that will attract the attention of the user and it is the deciding factor on whether the user will remember the website. I also enjoy the fact that I'm in control of how the website will look like. There is a infinite amount of possibility on how a website can be put together. That's what I like about Front-End development: bringing my design to life.

I am a Software Eng Student at Concordia University and I'm enrolled in the Co-op Program. I am always eager to learn new tools for development so that I can expand my knowledge and experience. If you have any ideas and think that I'm well suited for the position, contact me and let's talk about it!

I am also a huge gamer. Being a software engineer student, I hope to contribute to the gaming field in some way.
